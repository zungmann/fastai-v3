Classify Cat or Dog Breed

This work is a fork of https://github.com/render-examples/fastai-v3 and modified to classify cat or dog breed.

The classifier is ResNet34 trained on The Oxford-IIIT Pet Dataset (https://www.robots.ox.ac.uk/~vgg/data/pets/). The training used FastAI v1 and achieved 93.3% accuracy on validation set.
